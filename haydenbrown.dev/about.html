who is this guy anyway?

Hi, I'm Hayden Brown. Thanks for checking out my site! I'm a normal guy with a lot of hobbies, so this place is a tidy little public home for them.

I graduated Binghamton University in 2020, where I studied computer science and mathematics, co-led an undergrad research team that parsed viral DNA for string patterns, worked as a teaching assistant for various computer science courses, and occasionally spun the wheels as a radio DJ for the university's student-run FM station.

While the 2020 pandemic made my graduation year go a bit worse than I expected, I am still actively searching for work in a variety of technical industries, including data science, software engineering, research, bioinformatics, healthcare, statistics, games, and political outreach. I am a generalist and always up for a new challenge.
